# UI Redesign Complete: Order Completion Page

## ✅ What Changed

I have completely redesigned the **Order Completion Page** (`/orders/[id]/complete`) to match your requirements.

### 1. New Layout Structure
- **Top Section:** Split view with **Order Image** on the left and **Key Metrics** on the right.
- **Process Section:** New **Side-by-Side Grid Layout** for manufacturing processes.

### 2. Visual Improvements
- **Order Image:** Now prominently displayed to verify the item before completion.
- **Process Cards:** Each process (Filing, Polish, etc.) is shown in its own card with:
  - Process Name & Step Number
  - **Input Weight**
  - **Output Weight** (Blue)
  - **Loss** (Red)
  - Karigar Name
- **Responsive Design:** Adapts from 1 column (mobile) to 4 columns (desktop) for a perfect side-by-side view.

### 3. Better Summary
- **Gold Allocation:** Clear breakdown of allocated gold vs. loss.
- **Final Status:** Highlighted final weight and net gold weight.

## 🚀 How to View
1. Go to any **In-Progress Order**.
2. Click the **"Complete Order"** button (or navigate to `/orders/[id]/complete`).
3. You will see the new dashboard-style layout!
